# WodiBooks

> Reverse engineering distribution by writing and selling books.

## Why this exists

Right now I'm living on my last funds: ₦0.

Not "low funds."

Zero.

Like I'm just broke.

I've tried many ways of making money online.

- Building SaaS products
- Selling my development skills
- Building in public
- Contributing to open source
- Creating content

Every time I ran into the same wall:

Distribution.

I built a good product, but I couldn't get it into the hands of the right people at scale. Without enough users, there was no meaningful growth.

Eventually I realized the bottleneck wasn't building.

It was distribution.

## The mission

Until the end of 2026, I'm focusing on one skill:

Distribution.

Sales and marketing are simply parts of that larger skill.

My goal is to turn them from bottlenecks into strengths.

## The framework

I'm reverse engineering distribution from scratch.

```
Attention
    ↓
Interest
    ↓
Trust
    ↓
Users
    ↓
Retention
    ↓
Revenue
```

The plan is simple:

1. Master distribution at a small scale.
2. Understand what works, what doesn't, and why.
3. Apply those lessons to larger products, starting with ExplainThisRepo.

## Why books?

For the small-scale experiment, I've chosen to write and sell books.

It aligns with the kind of builder I'm becoming.

I enjoy expressing ideas, bringing my imagination to life, and creating something that inspire someone else.

## The goal

Over the next six months I want to:

- Write my first book.
- Sell 1,000 copies.
- Learn distribution through organic marketing.
- Reinvest the proceeds into paid marketing.
- Reinvest the profits back into building ExplainThisRepo.

The objective isn't just to sell books.

The objective is to build real distribution skill.

## Building in public

This repository documents the journey.

The failures.

The wins.

The lessons.

What actually moves attention.

What doesn't.
